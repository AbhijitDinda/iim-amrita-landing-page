Programme Overview

Amrita AHEAD MBA Online degree programme is offered by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, the 7th ranked university in India, and is backed by an industry-aligned curriculum and world-class faculty. Active participation from industry through certifications and projects are unique aspects of the Amrita AHEAD Online MBA course. The programme is also developed through a case study approach which develops the learner's problem-solving capability using various management principles and skills.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidates must have passed 10 + 2.
  • Candidates must have completed an undergraduate degree in Mathematics at 10 + 2 level or at graduation level.
  • Students in their final year can apply with the last completed semester results.
  • Candidates must have cleared the entrance test.
  • Interview with faculty is mandatory.
  • Final selection based on merit and performance.
